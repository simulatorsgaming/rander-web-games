🚀 Electron Dash – Dash Through the Stars

Get ready to zip through space tunnels with Electron Dash! Jump, dodge, and race as a glowing robot in a neon world. Watch out for lasers and gaps—it’s fast, fun, and will keep you on your toes!

🥚 Eggy Car – Don't Crack Under Pressure

Can you drive with a raw egg on your car? In Eggy Car, balance is everything. Go too fast, and the egg flies off! It's silly, fun, and oddly addictive. Great for laughs and learning patience.

🏍️ Moto X3M Winter – Ride Like a Pro

Zoom through snowy tracks, jump off icy ramps, and dodge wild obstacles in Moto X3M Winter. This stunt bike game is packed with explosions, flips, and frozen thrills!

🧠 Thief Puzzle – Use Your Brain

Thief Puzzle is not just fun—it’s smart! Use your stretchy arms to grab objects without getting caught. Every level is a little brain teaser that makes you think and laugh at the same time.

💥 Bad Time Simulator – A Real Challenge!

Think you have fast reflexes? Bad Time Simulator is inspired by the tough boss fights in Undertale. It’s tricky and intense—but super rewarding if you like games that test your focus and skill!

🎨 Color Rush – Match and Go!

Speed meets color in Color Rush. Drive through matching color walls and avoid crashing. Simple idea, fast action! Perfect for testing your reaction time and having quick bursts of fun.

🛣️ Slow Roads io – Take It Easy

Want a chill ride? Slow Roads io is a peaceful driving game with no goals—just relaxing music and beautiful scenery. Drive through nature or space and enjoy the calm.

🏈 2 Minute Football – Fast Football Fun

No time? No problem! 2 Minute Football is a super short and exciting football game where you pass, dodge, and score—all in just 2 minutes. Quick, fun, and great for sports fans!

🧱 MiniBlox – Build Your Dream World

MiniBlox lets your imagination run wild! Place blocks, build towers, dig tunnels—it’s your world. Just like Minecraft but easier to start and perfect for browser play.

⚽ Tricky Ball – Balance is Everything

Tricky Ball looks easy, but wait till you try it! Roll a ball over narrow tracks and tricky slopes without falling. It’s all about control, focus, and staying cool when the road gets hard.
